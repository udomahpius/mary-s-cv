About Me

Profile

Deputy Director- General & Head of Lagos Campus

Dr. Nasiru Tijani is a highly accomplished legal professional with over 34 years of experience. He is the Deputy-Director General and Head of the Lagos Campus at the Nigerian Law School. Dr. Tijani is an expert in litigation, matrimonial causes, and alternative dispute resolution. He has authored influential books on family law and constitutional law in Nigeria. Additionally, he is a Fellow of several prestigious organizations and serves as an editor for renowned legal publications. Dr. Tijani's expertise and contributions have solidified his standing as a respected figure in the legal community both nationally and internationally.

  • Fellow Institute of Chartered Mediators and Conciliators (FICMC)
  • Fellow Institute of Construction Industry Arbitrators (FICIArb)
  • Notary Public for Nigeria
  • Member, ICC Nigeria
